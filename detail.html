Creation Background

The protagonist of the work. A mysterious boy with a tail and innate super strength. Adopted by Grandpa Gohan, the senior disciple of Master Roshi, when he was young. After his grandfather passed away, he lived alone in the mountains, guarding the Four-Star Dragon Ball, an heirloom of Grandpa Gohan, diligently practicing martial arts every day, and enjoying fighting strong opponents.
