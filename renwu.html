Voice actors: Masako Nozawa (Japan), Wang Xiaoyan (China), Liu Yi (China)
The protagonist of the work. A mysterious boy with a tail and innate strength. Adopted by Master Roshi's senior disciple Grandpa Gohan when he was young. After Grandpa Gohan's death, he lived alone in the mountains, guarding the Four-Star Dragon Ball, an heirloom of Grandpa Gohan, practicing martial arts diligently every day, and enjoying fights with powerful opponents.

Bulma

Voice actors: Hiromi Tsuru (Japan), Wong Lai-Fong, Tsang Pui-Yee (VCD version)
The first companion Goku met in the deep mountains. The young lady of Capsule Corporation, who developed tools like the Dragon Radar, and is a genius in machinery.

Oolong

Voice actors: Naoki Tatsuta (Japan), Feng Yonghe, Chan Wing-Sun (Hong Kong, China)
An anthropomorphic pig-like Earthling. Initially, he was a villain who kidnapped beautiful girls but eventually reformed after a fight with Goku.

Yamcha

Voice actors: Toru Furuya (Japanese), Wong Kin-Keung (Cantonese), Zhang Wenyu (Mandarin)
Originally a desert bandit, he reformed after an encounter with Goku, Bulma, and others. Later became a disciple under Master Roshi.
